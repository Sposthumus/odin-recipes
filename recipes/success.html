SUCCESS! The Saul Goodman

It's all good, man - The perfect recipe for success

Who doesn't enjoy success?

Fortune and fame await those who follow these simple steps

Ingredients

  • One (1) bottle of Slippin' Jimmy
  • One (1) law degree - preferably from an offshore law school
  • Pallet(s) of disposable cellular devices/phones
  • Closet of fashionable suits - the more colorful the better

Instructions

  1. Place the law degree in a large mixing bowl
  2. Pour the bottle of Slippin' Jimmy into the bowl; stir as needed
  3. Let sit for 15 minutes, or until a law office has been opened in a strip mall
  4. Program the phone number of the newly formed law office into cellular devices
  5. Spread devices over a wide area - ideally to unscrupulous individuals
  6. Let said individuals cook up various, potentially criminal, ideas
  7. Apply fashionable suit(s) as dressing
