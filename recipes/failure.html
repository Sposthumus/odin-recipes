Failure; Jimmy (behind) Bars

Jump to recipe

Failure, or... redemption? How about both?

Jimmy (behind) Bars are just that.

A combination of a bad dude getting his just deserts, while also providing the refreshing sweetness of a redemption arc, long in the making, that dessert delivers.

Ingredients

  • One (1) guilty conscience
  • One (1) alter ego
  • One (1) extra long history of criminal behavior
  • One (1) endearing/charming personality

Instructions

  1. Place guilty conscience in large sauce pan and bring to a simmer
  2. Remove alter ego from original container and proceed to wrap it with the long history of criminal behavior; let sit until it hardens
  3. Once hardened, separate the two and discard the alter ego
  4. Reduce the heat on the guilty conscience and slowly stir in the endearing/charming personality
  5. After the two have been thoroughly stirred pour mixture into the hardened bar shaped mold of the extra long history of criminal behavior
  6. Let cool for extended period of time
  7. Serves approximately 30-life
